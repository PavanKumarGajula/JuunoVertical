Migration Night · April 6 – 9 · Before 7:00 PM

Do These 5 Things Before You Leave.

Close your day by 7:00 PM and follow these steps in order.

Step 01
🔄
Close All Apps & Restart
Close every open application, then reboot your computer and log back in.
Step 02
🛜
Stay Connected to Wi-Fi
Ensure your laptop is connected to the office network. Do not disconnect overnight.
Step 03
🔒
Lock Screen — Do NOT Log Out
Lock your screen only. Do not sign out or shut down. Sleep mode must be disabled.
Step 04
🔌
Plug Into Power
Keep your laptop plugged into power all night. It must not run on battery.
Step 05
💻
Lid Open — Leave It On
Leave your laptop lid open and powered on. It will reboot several times overnight.

Save Your Browser Passwords Before Your Migration
Passwords saved in Edge or Chrome will NOT migrate — they will be gone after migration.
